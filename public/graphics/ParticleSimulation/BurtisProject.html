Particle System Controls:
c or C key: toggle clear-screen. r/R key: soft/hard reset.
Space-bar: single-step. p or P key: pause/resume.
t/T key: less/more drag. g/G key: less/more gravity
v or V: switch solvers (implicit vs. explicit)
b or B: floor-bounce method


For Bouncy Balls: Press 'r' to launch

For Wind: Press 'z' to throw all particles up

For Spring: Hold 'y' to pull down
